Who We Are

Building a century of mornings.

We are more than just a bakery — we are a family of bakers committed to doing one thing properly: putting bread Barbados can rely on into every basket, every morning. From a single Bridgetown oven in 1923 to vans crossing every parish before dawn, the recipe for what matters hasn't changed.
