Our Heritage

Baking for Barbados since 1923.

For over a century, Zephirin's has stood as a pillar of Bajan morning rituals. Founded on Roebuck Street in Bridgetown, our ovens have run daily, bringing warmth and quality to kitchens across the island.

Our Journey

A century of bread, proven in time.

1923 — THE BEGINNING

Opening the First Ovens

Zephirin's Bakery opened its doors in Bridgetown on Roebuck Street. With one stone-deck oven and a commitment to using high-quality wheat and local sugars, we set the benchmark for authentic Barbadian Salt Bread.

1960 — EXPANSION

Supermarket Deliveries Begin

As the wholesale retail landscape in Barbados evolved, Zephirin's introduced daily delivery vans. We began dropping off fresh bread to corner shops, canteens, and the island's first supermarkets before dawn.

1995 — THE THIRD GENERATION

Family Recipes and New Bakes

The third generation of the family took over operations, expanding our line to include whole wheat specialties, hamburger rolls, and raisin bread, while strictly preserving our traditional slow-proof baking schedule.
