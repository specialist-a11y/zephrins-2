03 — BRAND PRINCIPLES

Design Philosophy

✦

Orange leads; yellow is a hint, never a flood.

Orange defines the brand warmth and call actions. Yellow is reserved for micro-accents, glowing radial details, or status icons.

✦

White space and big typography carry the energy.

Emphasize clean margins and extreme typographic hierarchies to convey confidence, quality, and modern design standards.

✦

Mono for facts; grotesque for voice.

Use IBM Plex Mono for technical data points, badges, coordinates, and hours. Use Archivo for editorial headings and descriptions.
